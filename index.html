Check red flags for authenticity

Red Flags to Watch For:

  • Blurry or unclear logos and labels
  • Missing batch or lot numbers
  • Inconsistent expiry date formatting
  • Misspelled drug name or manufacturer
  • Wrong color, shape, or size of tablets/capsules
  • Poorly printed or damaged packaging
  • No hologram or security features
  • Price significantly lower than market rate
  • Missing or unclear origin information
  • Inconsistent font sizes or spacing on label
  • Suspicious or unfamiliar certification marks
  • Spelling errors in ingredients or warnings
  • Unusual packaging materials or quality
  • Missing nutrition facts panel
  • Tampered or loose seals
  • No contact information for manufacturer
  • Color discrepancy from authentic product
  • Different texture or material feel
  • QR codes that don't work or link to wrong sites
  • Serial numbers that are easily scratched off
  • Packaging feels flimsy or lightweight
  • Graphics appear pixelated or low resolution
  • Different barcode format or placement
💡 Tip: Compare your product against an authentic one from an official retailer. Note any differences in packaging, weight, color, or feel.
